All my Interests

I like it all but here's a few big ones

Running

I've run run most of my life and gone throught cycles of love and hate with it. In high school I hated it. The entire experience was awful. Since being in college and having full freedom of how I train, I've grown to enjoy it a lot more.

Last October I ran the St George marathon. I was unable to train sufficiently because of an injury but enjoyed the race nonetheless. I made a goal there to run a marathon every year and am currently signed up for and training for the upcoming St George marathon.


Snowboarding

I take any opportunity to go snowboarding. Flowing throuh fresh powder is an unparalled feeling. I'm not great. There's not a ton of ski resorts around the east so I didn't get to go super often, but I'll send anything.

My favorite resort I've ever been to is Grand Targhee up in Wyoming. You get a view of the Tetons, a resort that isn't overly packed, and some dang good snow. I'm itching to get back up there.


F1

Now don't get confused. I'm not an F1 driver. I just like following along the seasons. I admire the drivers' dedication, the team aspect of the sport, and how it's international. It is unlike any other sport. Only the top 22 drivers in the world are racing in those cars. It's not just about who can drive the best. It's also about who's engineer can build the best performing car. To me, F1 is a nice contrast to the popular american sports that are filled with unintelligent athletes that are usually just very physically gifted.

This season of F1 has been pretty mediocre compared to previous seasons. I'm not a fan of the engine changes. We'll see how it pans out though.
